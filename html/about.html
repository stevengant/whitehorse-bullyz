About Us

Whitehorse Bullyz is a family owned and operated kennel. These dogs are our family members, not just pets! We strive for perfect temperaments and large-size dogs.

The American Bully

The American Bully is a loyal, loving and affectoinate dog breed. They make great family pets and are excellent with children, other dogs and other animals. Despite their powerful appearance, they are big teddy bears!
